Welcome to OmegleSpyX

OmegleSpyX is a freeware and opensource application that allows you to 'spy' on peoples conversations on Omegle. You can also send text as either user, block messages, disconnect users, and even swap a user without the other knowing. Some future plans include support for spying on video mode chats as well. I am always looking for good coders and other contributers to help with the OmegleSpyX project, see our webpage's 'Contibute' page.

Notice: Before you can start chatting you need to initialize OmegleSpyX. If the program feezes during the initialization process then please check your internet connection is working properly.











Visit OmegleSpyX's homepage at http://code.google.com/p/omeglespyx/
